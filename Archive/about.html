About

waiting on carmel sentence? This project is lead by Kate Macdonald, a vivid advocate and community educator. With the support of Leykey Beals, a web developer and youth worker.

Mission

Our mission is to create accessable black and queer history through art and story telling. By partnering with community members, we aim to document the living experiece of African Nova Scotian people.

How to get involved

If you something to share like stories, photos, music etc let us know!
